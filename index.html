About Me

To me, programming feels like solving puzzles and playing games. Piecing together code, hunting down bugs, DRYing up and optimizing the final product feel fulfilling, especially when users express appreciation for it and suggest features that they would like implemented.

My interest in programming started when I was 15. I dabbled with game scripts as a hobby, and in December 2018, I decided to hone my skills to pursue a career in the field. I have worked on several solo and group projects. You can read about my favorite ones on this site or go to my Github.
